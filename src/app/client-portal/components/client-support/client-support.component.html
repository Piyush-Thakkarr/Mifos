Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a payment?

You can make payments through our online portal, mobile app, or by visiting any of our branch offices.

What if I miss an EMI payment?

Please contact your loan officer immediately. We offer flexible repayment options to help you stay on track.

How can I check my loan balance?

Your current loan balance is always available on the Dashboard and Loans pages of this portal.

Can I prepay my loan?

Yes, you can prepay your loan at any time. Contact your loan officer for prepayment details and charges.
